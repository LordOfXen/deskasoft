Deskasoft Discord Moderator Guidelines

Last Update     Saturday, May 30, 2020 - 12:50 PM

We want to keep everything going at a quick pace and want to ensure that everything works smoothly out there. As we are going to publish Discord Moderator Applications as of Saturday, May 30, 2020, these guidelines are applicable for everyone becoming a moderator on Deskasoft.

Make sure to follow and familiarize yourself with the following rules. You agree to be bound by the following terms, and conditions by becoming a moderator.

Please make sure to familiarize yourself with our Community Guidelines located in our official Discord, here.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

You should never ask for personal information from the end-user without the written permission of Deskasoft International. In the event of violating this, we reserve the right to cancel and terminate your application at any time, without notice to you.

MINORS

This application is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. Providing wrong information will cause this application to be terminated at any time, without notice to you.

VIOLATIONS

Using your privileged moderator tools for scamming, disturbing, kicking or banning randomly, threatening, or insulting users will result in the termination of this application at any time, without notice to you.

CUSTOMERS

Responding roughly to the customers might cause issues on your application. You should always keep your nice behavior to customers regardless of their responses.

Remember, as long as you stay calm and behave nice, you'll always be the right side.

REPEATED OFFENDERS

Choose the offender's penalty by yourself, do not ban them immediately. For example, if they just send invites, warn at first, if they continue to do so; you can kick. If they join again to do the same, do not hesitate to ban, but avoid banning first.

CHANGES

We may update this guidelines from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

CONTACT US

For more information about our Moderator Guidelines, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at support@deskasoft.com or join our Discord server from here.
